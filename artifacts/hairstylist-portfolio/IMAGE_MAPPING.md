## RESTORATION WORKFLOW

### When You're Ready to Add Images Back:

1. **Prepare your edited images** with the exact filenames from this mapping
2. **Export each image in 4 formats:**
   - 3x4 portrait: 1200 × 1600 px
   - 4x5 portrait: 1280 × 1600 px
   - Featured landscape: 2400 × 1600 px
   - Social square: 1080 × 1080 px
3. **Place files in the correct folders** (see folder structure above)
4. **Special images:** hero-bg.png (3840×2160), about-portrait.png (1600×2400)
5. **Update data.ts** to point to the new image locations
