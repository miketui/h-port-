## ABOUT PAGE (/about)

**Pull Quote:** "Hair is never just hair."
**Section Header:** About Michael

**Bio Paragraph 1:** Some hairstylists build their careers behind the quiet rhythm of a salon chair. Michael David Warren built his inside the electric chaos of fashion's biggest stages.

**Bio Paragraph 2:** Rather than following the traditional path of salon apprenticeship, Michael stepped directly into the fast-moving world of fashion editorials and runway shows, assisting legendary hairstylists including Guido Palau, Jimmy Paul, and Jawara during major Fashion Weeks. Those early years placed him at the center of the creative engine that drives global fashion — where instinct, speed, and imagination are required in equal measure.

**Timeline Header:** Career Highlights
**Timeline Entries (12):**
- 2008–2012: Fashion Week Apprenticeships — Assisted Guido Palau, Jimmy Paul, and Jawara across New York, London, Milan, and Paris Fashion Weeks.
- 2012–2014: Mentorship & Development — Creative mentorship under Yusef Williams, Vernon François, and Naeemah LaFond shaped his cultural approach to hair.
- 2014: First Major Editorial — Landed his first significant independent editorial.
- 2015–2019: Rihanna Collaboration — Joined Rihanna's creative team, eventually becoming her day-to-day hairstylist.
- 2018: International Magazine Covers — Styled Duckie Thot for Harper's Bazaar, Wonderland, and Stylist UK covers.
- 2019: Savage X Fenty Show — Hair direction for Rihanna's Savage X Fenty runway show.
- 2019: Nike — Greatest Dynasty Ever — Lead hair stylist for Nike's landmark campaign.
- 2020–2021: Hollywood Expansion — Extended into major entertainment with Gugu Mbatha-Raw, Saniyya Sidney, and Elaine Welteroth.
- 2021: Grazia USA — Aurora Rising — Collaborated with Aurora James on her Grazia USA cover feature.
- 2022: Glamour Women of the Year — Styled Aurora James for Glamour's Women of the Year.
- 2022: Coveteur — Gottmik — Art-directed and styled the Gottmik Coveteur cover.
- 2023–Present: Creative Direction & Education — Developing new ventures in beauty innovation, education, and consulting.
